O’Tega draws from Africa’s natural wealth—rare spices, florals, resins and woods—infused into refined, contemporary fragrances that honor ancestral craftsmanship with modern elegance.

Our Founders

Mimshach Obiora, CEO

Born in Lagos and raised amidst Nigeria’s aromatic spice markets, Mimshach discovered his passion for scent in childhood. A chemical engineer by training, he later moved into fragrance creation, blending scientific precision with cultural storytelling to create O’Tega’s signature aroma.

Chidinma Nwadishi, COO

Chidinma grew up in the lush highlands of Ikeja, inspired by botanical essences. A trained perfumer and artist, her journey led her from community gardens to the heart of O’Tega’s atelier, championing ethical sourcing and artisanal techniques that make each fragrance a masterpiece.

Our Collection

Sahara Dusk

Amber and desert rose entwined with medicinal oud creates a bold yet warm scent evocative of Saharan twilight.

Forest Whisper

Savanna Spice

Coastal Bloom

Dusky Tindwa
